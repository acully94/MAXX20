Privacy Policy

MAXX20 HIIT

This Privacy Policy explains how MAXX20 HIIT ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the MAXX20 HIIT mobile application.

Information We Collect

MAXX20 HIIT does not require users to create an account. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, address, or payment details.

The app may collect limited, non-personal information to support app functionality and performance, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage and interaction data
  • Crash and performance diagnostics

How Information Is Used

Any collected information is used solely to:

  • Maintain and improve app performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand general usage patterns

Health & Fitness Data

MAXX20 HIIT does not collect, store, or share health or medical data. All workouts are self-directed, and users are responsible for their own training decisions.

Third-Party Services

The app may use standard third-party tools (such as Apple-provided analytics) to help understand app performance. These services are governed by their own privacy policies and do not provide us with personally identifiable data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

MAXX20 HIIT is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You may stop all information collection by uninstalling the app.
