MAXX20 HIIT

A focused 20-minute high-intensity interval training experience built for structure, simplicity, and maximum effort.

About the App

MAXX20 HIIT is designed for users who want efficient, time-based interval workouts. Sessions combine structured effort and recovery intervals, allowing users to train at their own intensity level.

The app provides guided interval timing and workout structure without unnecessary distractions.

Key Features

20-Minute Workouts

Efficient HIIT sessions designed to fit into busy schedules.

Structured Intervals

Clear timing for effort and recovery phases to help maintain focus.

Minimal & Focused

A clean interface that keeps attention on movement and effort.

Self-Paced Intensity

Suitable for different fitness levels by adjusting speed and incline.

Health & Safety

MAXX20 HIIT does not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified professional before beginning any new exercise program. Users are responsible for training within their personal limits.
